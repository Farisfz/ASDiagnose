    • Screening Test for Toddlers

    This test is a screening tool for toddlers age 36 months and below. It is designed to identify children who may benefit from a more thorough developmental and autism evaluation.
    It is important to note that the screening test may not detect early signs of autism and developmental delay in all children. If you have concerns about your child, talk to your pediatrician.
